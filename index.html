Share it: one file. Send rppg_monitor.html to anyone — they open it, drop in a video, and get results on their own laptop. Nothing is uploaded; all processing is local.
Best videos: face well-lit and fairly still, 15–60 s, looking toward the camera. The first reading needs a 10 s window, so use clips of at least ~12 s.
Live camera may be blocked when opening the raw file:// page; uploading a video always works, or serve it from http://localhost / any https:// URL.
⚠ Demonstration / wellness curiosity only — not a medical device, not for diagnosis.
